Why It Matters

Vineyard conditions change constantly and problems often become visible only after damage has already occurred. TerraGuard helps vineyard managers monitor large areas efficiently and gain early insights that support faster and better management decisions.

How It Works

TerraGuard uses drones to scan vineyards and collect aerial data about the crop. This data is processed to evaluate vineyard conditions. TerraGuard's AI assistant analyzes signals, detects potential risks or anomalies, and provides growers with clear insights for vineyard management.
