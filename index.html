Why It Matters

Vineyard conditions change constantly, and problems often become visible only after damage has started. TerraGuard helps vineyard managers monitor large areas efficiently and gain early insights that support faster and better management decisions.

How It Works

TerraGuard uses drones to scan vineyards and collect aerial data about the crop. This data is processed together with environmental information to understand conditions across the vineyard. The TerraGuard AI Co-Pilot analyzes these signals, identifies potential risks or anomalies, and provides growers with clear insights to support vineyard management decisions.
